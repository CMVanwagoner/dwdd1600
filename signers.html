Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
He was the principal author of the Declaration and is perhaps the most well-known signer. Later, he would become the third president of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned statesman, inventor, and diplomat, Franklin was one of the oldest signers at 70 years old.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, Adams was a key figure in the Continental Congress and would later serve as the second president of the United States.
Samuel Adams
Known for his role in organizing resistance to British policies, Samuel Adams was a prominent patriot and cousin to John Adams.
